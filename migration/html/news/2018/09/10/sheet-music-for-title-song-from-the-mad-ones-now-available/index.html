Sheet Music for Title Song from THE MAD ONES Now Available

September 10, 2018

If you caught THE MAD ONES (formerly The Unauthorized Autobiography of Samantha Brown) off-Broadway, you know that the run away hit of the show was the new title song. The Mad Ones is - now more than ever - focused on the friendship of Sam and Kelly and this song solidifies their chemistry.
