**World Premiere at La Jolla Playhouse** | August 19 - October 5, 2025

The Heart is a breathtaking new musical featuring an innovative electronic score that pulses with the rhythms of hospitals, nightclubs, the beach, and the human heart itself. The production marks the first musical fully based in electronic dance music (EDM).

**World Premiere Creative Team:**
- **Directed by** Christopher Ashley (Tony Award winner)
- **Choreography by** Mandy Moore (Taylor Swift's Eras Tour, La La Land)
- **Scenic Design:** Robert Brill
