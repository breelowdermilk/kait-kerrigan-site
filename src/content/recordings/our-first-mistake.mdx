Our First Mistake is Kerrigan-Lowdermilk's first album. It brings critically acclaimed and diverse artists from pop music and musical theater together onto one album. It spans styles and defies easy classification, illustrating Kerrigan-Lowdermilk's core philosophy that a good song is good in any genre. Guests include Michael Arden (The Times They Are A Changin' ), Matt Doyle (Bye Bye Birdie ), Meghann Fahy (Next To Normal ), Jay Armstrong Johnson (Hair ), Morgan Karr (Spring Awakening ), Kelli O'Hara (The Light In The Piazza ), Laura Osnes (South Pacific ) and Natalie Weiss (Everyday Rapture ).
